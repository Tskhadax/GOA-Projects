Kristina Carrillo-Bucaram, AKA "FullyRaw Kristina," is a writer, speaker and raw vegan activist who educates and guides people who are ready to take control of their health, and experience the compassionate lifestyle one can achieve by living FullyRaw. After switching to her raw vegan lifestyle and winning her battle over Type II Diabetes she is on a mission to share what she learned with others.
